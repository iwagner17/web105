I was born in Puebla, Mexico. I don't remember how it looks because I was 3 years old when my family moved to the U.S. The only memories I can make of it, are from the stories i've been told. This will be my first travel destination, because i want to connect with the culture that my family is associated to.

Publa is located in the southern part of Mexico. It's a colonial styled town because it was colonized by the Spaniards. Puebla has a total of 365 churches. It's mostly known for it's Cinco de Mayo battle. On May 5th,1862 Mexico defeated the French.
